King's Workshop

Player's Guide

Getting Started Resources Troops Bosses Prestige Tips

Build an Army. Defeat the Kings. Rule the Workshop.

King's Workshop is an incremental clicker game. Click to gather resources, spawn and equip soldiers, then send your army to battle escalating bosses. Automate your empire, prestige for permanent power, and push deeper than any king before you.

⚡ Getting Started

The core loop in three steps

🪨

1. Click for Scrap

Click the scrap piles in the Scrapyard to earn Nuts & Bolts — the game's primary currency.

🪖

2. Spawn Soldiers

Click the forge in the Workshop to accumulate spawn progress. Each soldier costs more clicks than the last.

⚔️

3. Equip & Fight

Spend Nuts & Bolts in the Armoury to equip your soldiers. Your army then fights bosses automatically.

Soldier Spawn Cost

Spawning soldiers gets harder as your army grows — each new soldier costs more clicks than the last. Your 10th soldier costs ~20 clicks; your 50th costs ~295; your 100th costs ~8,676. This is why automation matters.

🔩 Resources

Nuts & Bolts — the lifeblood of your kingdom

🖱️ Scrap Clicking

  • Each click on a scrap pile yields 1 N&B to start.
  • Upgrades can raise the Scrap Value multiplier, increasing yield per click.
  • There is a critical click chance (starts at 1%) that yields 5× the normal value — watch for the flash!

🤖 Collectors

Collectors are automated units that gather N&B without any clicking. They travel between scrap piles and the central stockpile, filling up and depositing continuously.

Attribute Base Effect
Speed 1 N&B/sec Collection rate
Capacity 10 units Units held before depositing
Count 1 More collectors in parallel

Upgrade all three attributes in the Scrapyard panel. Count upgrades are expensive but dramatically increase throughput.

🌙

Offline Progression

Collectors keep working while you're away, up to a cap of 8 hours. When you return, the game calculates what you earned and deposits it instantly. Offline efficiency starts at 50% and can be improved through the Research Tree after your first prestige.

🛡️ Troops

Four specialisations, each with a unique role

Newly spawned soldiers are unequipped and cannot fight. Visit the Armoury to assign them to an equipment station. Equipment cost scales exponentially with how many of that type you've already equipped.

🗡️ Infantry Base cost: 10 N&B

Sword & Shield. Your front-line tanks absorbing hits so your other troops can deal damage freely.

  • Damage type: Melee (sustained)
  • Role: Tank / front line
  • Special ability: Taunt (draws boss attacks)
🏹 Archer Base cost: 100 N&B

Bow & Arrow. Reliable single-target damage dealers who operate safely from the back line.

  • Damage type: Ranged (single target)
  • Role: DPS / back line
  • Special ability: Volley (multi-shot burst)
🐴 Cavalry Base cost: 1,000 N&B

Horse & Spear. Heavy shock troops that deal massive burst damage on the charge — expensive but devastating.

  • Damage type: Charge (burst)
  • Role: Flanker / burst damage
  • Special ability: Charge (high burst on entry)
🔮 Mage Base cost: 10,000 N&B

Staff & Spellbook. Elite support casters with area-of-effect magic — a major investment with major payoff.

  • Damage type: AoE magic
  • Role: Support / area damage
  • Special ability: Fireball (area explosion)

Troop Upgrade Trees

Every troop type has three upgrade branches, each purchasable with N&B:

⚔️ Damage

Increases base damage output per troop of this type.

🛡️ Armour / Health

Increases survivability so your troops last longer against tough bosses.

✨ Special Ability

Unlocks and upgrades the troop's unique ability — often the biggest power spike.

📜

Blueprints & Rare Equipment

Blueprints drop from Elite and Legendary bosses. Each blueprint unlocks a unique equipment variant for one troop type. Combining matched blueprints (e.g. Flaming Sword + Enchanted Shield on Infantry) grants powerful synergy bonuses — up to +25% damage for that troop type.

👹 Boss Battles

The progression gates of the kingdom

How Battles Work

Your army fights bosses automatically. You can click during a battle to deal bonus damage and speed things along. Each boss has a health pool and deals damage to your troops — a bigger army is always better.

Boss difficulty increases exponentially — both HP and damage grow steeply with each encounter.

Boss Rewards

Defeating a boss drops N&B — and the rewards scale even faster than the difficulty, ensuring you always make progress. By mid-game, boss rewards will far outpace what clicking can earn, making boss fights your primary income source.

👺

Standard Boss

Every boss. Normal HP and damage. Drops standard N&B reward.

👿

Elite Boss (every 10th)

3× normal HP, unique mechanics, 5× reward + random Blueprint drop.

💀

Legendary Boss (every 50th)

10× HP, 20× reward, guaranteed rare Blueprint + Prestige Token.

👑 Prestige: The Royal Decree

Reset to grow stronger — permanently

When progress slows, issue a Royal Decree to prestige. This resets your soldiers, equipment, collectors, and boss progress — but grants Royal Crowns, a permanent meta-currency. The more Nuts & Bolts you've earned over the lifetime of that run, the more Crowns you receive.

Throne Room Upgrades

Spend Royal Crowns on permanent multipliers that carry through every prestige:

⚒️ Royal Forge

−5% all equipment costs per level

💰 King's Treasury

+10% all resource income per level

📣 Master Recruiter

−1 soldier spawn click per level (min 2)

⚔️ Battle Hardened

+15% all troop damage per level

🏃 Swift Couriers

+8% collector speed per level

🔬

Research Tree

Unlocked after your first prestige. Spend Royal Crowns to unlock new global mechanics:

  • Offline progression (collectors work while you're away)
  • Auto-spawn (soldiers spawn automatically over time)
  • Critical click chance improvements
  • Collector pathfinding optimisation
  • Troop synergy bonuses

💡 Tips & Strategy

Make the most of your kingdom

1️⃣

Invest in Collectors early

Clicking will plateau fast. Even a few Collector upgrades unlock a reliable passive income that compounds over time — prioritise these before you try to equip lots of troops.

2️⃣

Frontload Infantry, then diversify

Infantry are cheap and give your army tankiness to survive tougher bosses. Once you can comfortably defeat bosses, branch into Archers for consistent DPS, then Cavalry for burst power.

3️⃣

Click during boss fights

Your clicks deal bonus damage directly to the boss. On Elite and Legendary bosses especially, active clicking can shave significant time off a fight — and those bosses have the best loot.

4️⃣

Prestige before you stall

Don't wait until you're completely stuck. Prestige when you have enough lifetime N&B to earn a meaningful number of Royal Crowns — even 3–5 Crowns can dramatically speed up your next run.

5️⃣

Unlock the Research Tree fast

Your first prestige unlocks the Research Tree. Offline progression and auto-spawn from the tree transform how quickly subsequent runs play out — getting to your first Royal Decree is a major turning point.

6️⃣

Chase Blueprint synergies

When you find matching Blueprints for the same troop type, equipping both activates a synergy bonus. These can be more powerful than many direct upgrades — keep an eye on your Blueprint collection.
