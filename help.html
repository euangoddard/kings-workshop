Player's Guide

Build an Army. Fortify Your Kingdom. Defeat the Bosses.

King's Workshop is an incremental clicker game. Click to gather resources, spawn and equip soldiers, build turrets, and send your army to battle escalating bosses. Automate your income, upgrade your defences, and unleash devastating special attacks at the critical moment.

⚡ Getting Started

The core loop in three steps

🔩

1. Click for Scrap

Click Collect Scrap in the Scrapyard panel to earn Nuts & Bolts — the game's only currency.

🪖

2. Spawn Soldiers

Spend N&B in the Workshop to spawn soldiers. Each new soldier costs a little more than the last.

⚔️

3. Equip & Fight

Spend N&B in the Armoury to equip your soldiers as troops. Your army then fights bosses automatically.

Soldier Spawn Cost

Each soldier costs floor(10 × 1.07n) N&B. Cost grows steadily as your army grows — this is why automating income through Collectors and Turrets matters.

🔩 Resources

Nuts & Bolts — the lifeblood of your kingdom

🖱️ Scrap Clicking

  • Each click yields 1 N&B to start.
  • Upgrade Click Power to earn more N&B per click.
  • There is a Critical Click chance (starts at 1%) that multiplies the click value by 5×. Watch for the flash!
  • Upgrade Crit Chance to land crits more often.

🤖 Collectors

Collectors are automated units that gather N&B passively. Upgrade them in the Workshop panel (toggle the Collector tab).

Attribute Base Effect per upgrade
Speed 1 N&B/sec +0.5 N&B/sec
Capacity 10 units ×2 capacity
Count 1 +1 collector

Count upgrades are expensive but multiply your total throughput directly.

🛡️ Troops

Four specialisations — each stronger and costlier than the last

Newly spawned soldiers are unequipped — visit the Armoury to assign them to a troop type. Equipment cost scales exponentially with how many of that type you already have. Each troop type deals passive DPS to the boss every second while a battle is active.

🛡️ Infantry Base equip: 10 N&B · 5 DPS

Your cheapest front-line troops. Low damage individually but easy to mass-produce early.

🏹 Archer Base equip: 100 N&B · 12 DPS

Solid ranged damage dealers. A reliable mid-game investment once you have a stable income.

🐴 Cavalry Base equip: 1,000 N&B · 20 DPS

Expensive but deal significantly more DPS. Worth investing in once collectors provide steady N&B.

🔮 Mage Base equip: 10,000 N&B · 35 DPS

Elite casters with the highest base DPS. A serious late-game investment with serious payoff.

Troop Upgrade Branches

Each troop type has three upgrade branches. Costs double with every level.

⚔️ Damage

+15% damage multiplier per level for this troop type.

❤️ Health

+20% health multiplier per level — increases survivability.

✨ Special

Reserved for future abilities. Purchase now to stay ahead.

👊

Unequipped Soldiers & Click Damage

During a boss fight, clicking Fight! deals bonus instant damage: 10 + 1 per unequipped soldier. Keeping some soldiers unequipped is a deliberate trade-off — they buff your click damage but don't contribute passive DPS.

🏰 Turrets

Permanent fortifications that deal automatic damage to bosses

What Are Turrets?

Turrets are stationary weapons built in the Scrapyard panel (switch to the 🏰 Turrets tab). Once built, each turret deals continuous passive DPS to the boss during every battle — stacking with your army's damage.

Unlike troops, turrets don't require soldiers to build. They're a parallel power track funded purely by N&B.

Building & Upgrading

Action Cost formula
Build Turret floor(200 × 2.5n) — each turret costs more
Upgrade Turrets floor(500 × 3.0level) — upgrades all at once

Turret upgrades apply to every turret you own, so upgrading after building several turrets is especially efficient.

DPS Formula

Each turret deals 10 × 1.5level DPS. At Lv 0 that's 10 DPS/turret; at Lv 3 it's ~33.75 DPS/turret. Turret DPS scales multiplicatively with the Troop Buff special attack.

👹 Boss Battles

The progression gates of the kingdom

How Battles Work

Your army and turrets fight bosses automatically once you press Challenge. The combined DPS whittles the boss down every game tick.

You can click Fight! at any time to deal instant bonus damage — useful for finishing off a tough boss quickly.

Boss Scaling

Stat Formula
HP floor(100 × 2.5n)
Reward floor(50 × 3.0n)

Rewards scale faster than HP — by mid-game, boss rewards dwarf what clicking can earn.

👺

Standard Boss

Every boss. Normal HP and reward.

👿

Elite Boss (every 10th)

3× HP, 5× reward. Marked with ⚡ in the UI.

⚡ Special Attack

A charged bar that turns the tide of battle

Charging the Bar

The Special Attack bar appears in the battle screen once a boss fight starts. It fills automatically while the battle is active and gains a burst of charge whenever you click Fight!

Source Charge gained
Passive (battle active) +5% per second
Manual Fight! click +3% per click

The bar requires 80% charge before either ability can be used.

💥

Burst Attack

Deals 5× your current total DPS as instant damage to the boss. Total DPS includes army DPS, any active buff, and all turret DPS.

Best used when a boss is nearly dead, or to skip a particularly long grind on an Elite boss.

🛡️

Troop Buff

Doubles your army's DPS for 15 seconds. Turret DPS is unaffected — only troop damage is multiplied. A countdown timer shows the remaining duration.

Best used at the start of a boss fight, or right before a Burst Attack to maximise its damage value.

💡 Tips & Strategy

Make the most of your kingdom

1️⃣

Invest in Collectors early

Clicking income plateaus quickly. A few Collector upgrades (Speed + Count) unlock reliable passive N&B that compounds — prioritise these before trying to equip lots of troops.

2️⃣

Build turrets alongside your army

Turrets don't need soldiers — they're a parallel upgrade track. Even a single Lv 0 turret deals 10 DPS constantly. Upgrading turrets after building a few is especially efficient since the bonus applies to all of them.

3️⃣

Use the Troop Buff before a Burst Attack

Burst Attack damage is based on your current total DPS — including any active buff. Activate Troop Buff first, then immediately fire Burst Attack to deal up to 10× your normal DPS as instant damage.

4️⃣

Keep some soldiers unequipped

Each unequipped soldier adds +1 to your Fight! click damage. Holding a handful back means your active clicks hit harder — a worthwhile trade on boss fights that drag on.

5️⃣

Click during boss fights to charge the bar faster

Each Fight! click adds 3% to the Special Attack bar on top of the passive 5%/sec charge. On an Elite boss, spamming clicks can get you multiple activations in a single fight.

6️⃣

Frontload Infantry, then diversify

Infantry are cheap to equip. Once you have a stable income from Collectors, branch into Archers (12 DPS), then Cavalry (20 DPS), and eventually Mages (35 DPS) for the biggest damage spikes.
